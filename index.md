I am a PhD Candidate in Demography at the University of Pennsylvania. 

I hold an M.A. in Statistics and Data Science from the Wharton School of University of Pennsylvania, and a M.Sc. in Demography, and a B.Sc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Campinas.

My research has been devoted to the application of demographic methods for mortality and fertility estimation in low- and middle-income countries of Latin America. In my thesis I explore the *Demography of Under-5 Mortality* .
